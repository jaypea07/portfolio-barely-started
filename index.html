I'm a web developer based in Dallas, Texas with a passion for problem-solving and an eye for detail.

Growing up in the quiet suburbs of Vancouver, Canada, my passion for web design started when I was nine. I wanted to go to summer camp; my mom signed me up for a computer day camp instead. It was there that I created my first web page and I've been picking my way through code ever since.

In May 2012, I graduated from the University of Texas at Arlington with a bachelor's degree in Communication Technology—their fancy-shmancy way of saying web development. I'm currently a Front End Engineer at Bottle Rocket Apps and will occassionaly take on freelance work.
